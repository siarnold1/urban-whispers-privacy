# Privacy Policy — Urban Whispers

**Effective date:** July 4, 2026

Urban Whispers ("the App", "we") is an audio travel guide app. We built it to work without collecting personal information. This policy explains what little data the App processes, why, and what your rights are.

---

## 1. The Short Version

- **No account required.** The App works without registration — we never ask for your name, email address, or any other personal details.
- **No location tracking.** The App does not access, collect, or store your GPS location.
- **No advertising trackers.** We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
- **Anonymous usage statistics only.** We record which features are used (e.g. an episode was played, a quiz was started) under a random identifier that is not linked to you as a person.

---

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, request, or store:

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses
- GPS or precise location data
- Contacts, photos, or files on your device
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA) — the App does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not track you

---

## 3. Data We Do Process

### 3.1 Anonymous Usage Events

To understand how the App is used and to improve our content, the App records simple usage events, for example:

- An episode was started, played, or completed
- A quiz was started
- A quiz question was answered correctly or incorrectly
- A badge was earned

Each event is stored together with a **randomly generated installation identifier**. This identifier is created when you first install the App, contains no personal information, and is not linked to your name, email, device serial number, or any other identity. If you delete and reinstall the App, a new random identifier is generated.

We use this data in aggregate form only — for example, to see which episodes are popular or which quiz questions are too difficult.

**Storage location:** Our database is hosted by Supabase Inc. on servers located in **Frankfurt, Germany (EU)**.

### 3.2 Crash Reports

If the App crashes, a technical crash report is sent to Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) so we can find and fix the problem. Crash reports contain:

- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Technical information about the error (stack trace)

Crash reports are configured to **exclude personal information** (no IP-based user profiles, no names, no identifiers linked to you). Crash data is processed on Sentry's **EU infrastructure (Germany)**.

### 3.3 Data Stored Only on Your Device

Your bookmarks, listening progress, downloaded episodes, and similar preferences are stored **locally on your device only**. This data never leaves your phone and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 4. Why We Process This Data (Legal Basis)

We process anonymous usage events and crash reports based on our **legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in operating, securing, and improving the App. Because the data is not linked to your identity, the impact on your privacy is minimal.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not sell** data and do **not share** it with advertisers. Data is processed only by the service providers named above (Supabase for hosting, Sentry for crash reporting), who act as our processors under data processing agreements and may not use the data for their own purposes.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- **Usage events:** retained for as long as needed for product analytics, then deleted or fully aggregated.
- **Crash reports:** automatically deleted by Sentry after the standard retention period (90 days).
- **Local data on your device:** under your control — deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of personal data, the right to object, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Please note: because the data we hold is identified only by a random installation ID, we usually **cannot link any stored data to you as a person** — and therefore cannot look up "your" data unless you provide us with your installation ID. You can effectively reset all analytics data associated with your installation by uninstalling and reinstalling the App.

---

## 8. Children

Urban Whispers does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. The App is suitable for general audiences.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we add features in the future that change how data is handled (for example, optional accounts or location-based features), we will update this policy before those features launch and indicate the new effective date at the top of this page.
